Legal Compliance: We adhere to all relevant child protection laws, including reporting obligations and content restrictions. Monitoring and Reporting: We implement robust monitoring systems to detect and remove any violating content promptly and report it to the appropriate authorities. Collaboration: We cooperate fully with law enforcement and child protection agencies to support investigations and protect children. User Agreement and Terms of Service By using Donikkah, users agree to abide by our strict policies against child abuse and exploitation. Reporting Mechanisms Users can report any suspected child abuse content or behavior directly through our in-app reporting tools or by contacting our support team.
